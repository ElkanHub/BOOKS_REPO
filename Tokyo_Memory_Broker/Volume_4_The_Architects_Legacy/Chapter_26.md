# Chapter 26: The Choice of History

They weren't "Syncing" with the Loom. They were "Drafting" a future. 

"The promotion is complete, Neo-Tokyo," Ren whispered, his voice sounding through the static. 

He didn't raise his Spike-Knife. He didn't have to. 

He looked at the final ballot on the CRT monitor—not a clinical-white reader, but a tattered piece of Syndicate hardware that had just shown him his own cost.
