# Chapter 07: The Election Call

The "Election Call" wasn't a broadcast; it was a "Neuro-Sync"—a high-frequency burst of raw, unedited trauma that turned the city into a single, collective migraine. 

Ren Sato stood in the center of the ruins of the Kanda Tower’s Service-Plinth, his hand on a heavy, tactile-interface that smelled of dust and unedited logic. Beside him, Hana was breathing in shallow, rhythmic bursts, her Siphon pulsing a dim, exhausted blue. 

"The promotion is complete, Neo-Tokyo," Ren whispered, his voice sounding through the static. 

He didn't use a resonance stave. He used the "Filter-Node" from the sub-basement, its obsidian surface geysering sparks and blue electricity into the rain. 

*SECURITY PROTOCOL 'ARCHITECT-OVERRIDE' ENGAGED.* 
*BROADCAST: THE-ELECTION-OF-ECHOES.* 

"The promotion is over," the woman from the council roared—not from the terminal, but from a thousand voices combined. 

The image of the Director’s professional clipboard filled the sky—not a "Hero-Vault" record, but a clinical white screen. 

"We've seen the cost!" the people roared, their voices sounding like a threat. 

"We've felt the pain of the 'Quiet-Death' orphanage! We've heard the sound of the children’s laughter turning to static! And now? Now we choose which memories to keep!"
