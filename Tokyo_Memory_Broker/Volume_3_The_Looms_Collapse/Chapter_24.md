# Chapter 24: The Neutralizer-Grid

The "Neutralizer-Grid" wasn't a piece of hardware; it was a "Collective-Sync"—a high-frequency burst of raw, unedited trauma and hope that turned the ruins of the Shibuya station into a single, light-swallowing laboratory. 

Hana stood in the center of the square, her Siphon pulsing a dim, exhausted blue. Around her, a dozen hollow-resonance units were scavengeing from the ruins of a mag-lev train, their hands geysering sparks and blue electricity into the dark. 

They weren't "Truth-Wave" technicians anymore. They were "Day-One" architects—donors who had just realized that the Nihilists' "Year-Zero" was a cage they were willing to die to shatter. 

"The promotion is complete, Child," a voice smoothed into Hana’s brain—not Ren’s voice, but a collective, synthesized voice that sounded like a thousand voices combined. 

The Grey Collective. 

"I'm not the Director anymore, Miki!" Hana screamed, her lungs feeling like they were filled with dry ice. 

She wasn't carrying a resonance stave. She was carrying a single, tattered photo—the "Analog-Anchor" from the Sector 7 school. 

Opposite her, Miki lead the Nihilist’s "Null-Guards." These wasn't the "Static-Headed" addicts anymore. These were the "Archive-Deleters"—donors who had just realized that their own history was a debt they were willing to 'Clear' by deleting the human soul. 

"Yield, Girl! No Past. No Pain!" Miki cried, her lopsided smile matching the photo, but her eyes devoid of any human recognition. 

"The saving is a lie, Hana-chan! You're the one who told us that a single memory of a mother's smile was worth more than a dozen 'Blank-Slate' futures. But the 'Debt-Wipe' doesn't just clear the books; it clears the ground! We don't want a history! We want the 'Void'!" 

Miki lunged. 

She didn't use her weapon. She used the momentum of her own guilt, slamming into her old friend with the force of a mag-lev collision. She felt the weight of Miki’s pulse, the rhythmic, violent energy of her "Null-Script." 

"Miki, stop! It's me! It's Hana!" 

Hana feel the weight of the city’s gaze. She could feel the thousands of eyes in the Grey Zone, all staring at her—at the woman who was currently holding the code to their own sanity. 

*SECURITY PROTOCOL 'NEUTRALIZER-GRID' ANALYZED.* 
*STATUS: COLLECTIVE SENSORY-RESTORE v0.0* 
*FUNCTION: NIHILIST-PULSE NEUTRALIZATION.*

But Ren didn't answer.
